About

Hi, I’m Kundan.

I’m a passionate Full Stack Developer who loves turning ideas into clean, efficient, and scalable web applications. With hands-on experience in Node.js, React, Next.js, MongoDB, MySQL, REST APIs, and TailwindCSS, I build solutions that not only perform well but also delight users with smooth and intuitive interfaces. I focus on blending thoughtful design with solid engineering to create applications that truly make an impact. Ability to learn new software tools and technologies quickly. Capability to work in teams by providing valuable support. Eager to work on challenging projects.

One of my favorite accomplishments has been creating backend systems that handle complex data efficiently and integrating them smoothly with modern frontends. I enjoy tackling challenges—whether optimizing performance, ensuring reliable API communication, or crafting flexible UI designs with React and TailwindCSS. My versatility lets me contribute across the stack and help bring projects to life.

When I’m not coding, I love contributing to open-source projects, learning new things, and connecting with others who share a passion for technology. If you’ve got a project or an idea you want to explore, let’s connect and create something amazing together!
